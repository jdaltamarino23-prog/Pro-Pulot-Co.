About Pro Pulot Co.

Who We Are


Pro Pulot Co. is more than a pickleball team. We are a growing community of players who inspire one another to become better athletes, stronger teammates, and lifelong friends. Founded in 2026, our organization welcomes players of every level while promoting discipline, respect, sportsmanship, and passion for the game.

Our Story


What started as a simple gathering of friends quickly evolved into one of Dumaguete City's passionate pickleball communities. Our members continuously support one another through friendly competition, tournaments, community outreach, and unforgettable moments on and off the court.

Our Motto


"We Don't Just Play, We Pulot."


More than winning games, we build friendships, create memories, and grow together as one family.

Mission

To build an inclusive pickleball community that promotes excellence, sportsmanship, teamwork, discipline, and healthy living while inspiring every member to continuously improve both on and off the court.

Vision

To become one of the most respected and influential pickleball communities in the Philippines by fostering friendship, competitive excellence, leadership, and a lifelong passion for the sport.
